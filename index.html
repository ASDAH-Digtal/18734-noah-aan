Kuroko's Basketball

Kuroko's basketball is a anime based on a high school kid named Tetsuya Kuroko who plays basketball. He teams up with a player named Taiga kagami an american basketball player who comes to play in japan. They join in a team named serin and try to become the best team in Japan. He ends up versing people from his old team called the generation of miracles. This team was a team who was the best in middle school. They ended up spliting up to make thier own team stronger and prove that they are best in whole of japan.
